Concept Design

After the initial client meeting where goals and objectives are agreed up and discussed, the first step in the interior design process is concept development.

We will research and prepare styles and images that we think will be suitable for the space. We use this time to prepare our initial style concept boards that we use as a visual tool to clarify the style and confirm the brief between the designer and client.

Developed Design

Once you are happy with the concept design we will develop the detailed design, Producing detailed drawings, such as layout, joinery drawings and bathroom drawings and sourcing the furniture pieces which will all be presented to you along with a detailed budget for sign off.
