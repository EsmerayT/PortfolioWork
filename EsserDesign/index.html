Interior Designer

DOMINIQUE ESSSER

Dominique strives to design unique interiors that will have a positive impact on her clients’ lives. She is passionate about delivering effective and innovative design solutions and finding creative ways to help budget-conscious clients design their dream spaces. Dominique offers a balance of creativity, collaboration and forward thinking with a structured, organised and detailed mentality; enabling her to deliver focused development, follow through and total completion to each aspect of the design. With a previous career in Marketing and a degree in Business Science, Dominique is a confident designer with a sound business head on creative shoulders In her spare time, she enjoys spending time with her friends and family, exploring the city’s cultural, art, and food scene, as well as traveling worldwide for experiences and inspiration.










Esser Design is an interior design studio based in central London. We deliver unique design solutions for residential and commercial projects in Britain and across the world. Meticulous attention to detail and a thorough understanding of how people live are at the heart of all of our projects.






















ESSER DESIGN STUDIO

Our mission is to create habitable spaces with identity. Whether it’s a home, a restaurant, a hotel or a commercial development, we apply the same creative approach that starts with envisioning the lives of the people who will inhabit it. We question what inspires them, what moves them and how we can fulfil their desires and support their needs with our design.
